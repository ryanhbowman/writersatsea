At
Sea

Two one-week residencies for young writers in Margate. Recipients will have use of a historic two bedroom seaside flat in Margate as well as a £250 bursary towards food and £50 towards train travel.

Each year of the residency is themed, with the inaugural theme being very general – novel-writing. Applicants should be in the midst of a novel and will need to submit 10,000 words plus a synopsis with their application.
