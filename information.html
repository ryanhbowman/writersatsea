The At Sea Residency program aims to provide a quiet, and quietly inspiring, place for young writers to conentrate solely on writing and thinking without the distractions and pressures of the every day. Writing is a endeavor that rewards persistence, time and concentration. At Sea hopes spectacular sunsets, a vibrant sea-side culture and, above all, space, will help.

Accomodation

You will have full run of a two bedroom, two bathroom Grade-II listed flat in the old Fort Lodge hotel built in 1823. The flat is at the top of a majestic sprialing staircase and is filled with hundreds of books for reference and fun. There is a full kitchen wth all the living and cooing basics. You will be provided, of course, with sheets and towels.

Stipend

As well as having private access to a peaceful seafront apartment, the successful applicant will be given a £250 stipend in cash. This should be enough to cover food for the week, as well as a little local sightseeing for inspiration. If you are travelling to Margate by train, we can also pay £50 towards your train fare.

Dates

The inaugural At Sea Residency will run from Saturday 17 November 2018 to Sunday 25 November 2018. This first residency will be open to novel writers. The second installment will run from Saturday 9 March 2019 to Sunday 17 March 2019, and will be open to playwrights.

Selection Criteria

If you are under 30 and you write, you are eligible to apply for At Sea. However, the theme of the residency will change each time: the first residency (in November 2018) will be open to anyone under 30 who is working on a novel; the second (in March 2019) is open to anyone under 30 who is working on a play. (Poets, memoirists, graphic novelists, screenwriters, biographers, essayists - your time will come!)

You don't need to be 'established' in your career, or have been published - we are looking for promising, passionate writers who will relish a week of solitude to devote to their work. We do expect that you have put some time and energy into your writin already so we ask for a short excerpt from the work you plan on honin while you are in Margate.

We especially welcome applications from writers from diverse or lower-income backgrounds.

Application Process

We would like to read a sample of your writing - ideally the project you plan to work on during the residency. We also want to hear about you: please provide a few paragraphs about yourself, your background, your creative interests, and why At Sea would be helpful to you.
